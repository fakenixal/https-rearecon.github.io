About Rear Econ

We help young economists grow through real work, real mentorship, and real opportunities.

Hands‑on Research Training

We teach students to work with real data, real tools, and real economic questions.

Mentorship That Matters

We connect emerging researchers with mentors who guide their academic and professional growth.

Applied Research Projects

We collaborate on studies that give students real experience beyond the classroom.

A Growing Research Community

We create spaces where young economists meet, learn, and discover opportunities.

Writing & Publishing Support

We help researchers write clearly, present confidently, and publish their work.
