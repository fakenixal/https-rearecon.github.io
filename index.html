About Rear Econ

How we think and how we work.

Rear Econ is built on a simple premise: economic analysis should be rigorous enough to stand up to scrutiny and clear enough to guide real decisions.

Our work spans policy design, market analysis, and impact evaluation — but the core remains the same: careful reasoning, transparent methods, and outputs that are actually usable.
